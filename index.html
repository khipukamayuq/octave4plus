Extend your range

We are the first to offer octave zero strings for the 28.625"-30" scale with bass and guitar ball ends. B0, the same low B on a 5-string bass is available for guitars like the Agile Intrepid Pro, Halo 10-string and Oakland Axe Factory ERGs. We can even make standard bass guitar E1 for 25.5" scale guitar.

Tune up to A440 Hz

If you have tried to tune up to A4, then you know the all too familiar "snap" before you reach A440 Hz. Some believe that a thinner gauge string will automatically tune to a higher pitch. This is not the case. Octave4Plus strings are pitch specific for each scale length.
